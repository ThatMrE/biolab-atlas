Notes

Activity statuses come from a May 2026 verification pass: ~40 labs were individually checked via web fetch + search; remaining labs were classified by URL-pattern heuristics. Click any marker to see the evidence cited for its status. If you spot stale data, submit a correction with the same lab name and we'll merge it.
